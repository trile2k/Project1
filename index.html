Welcome to Tri Le's Project-1 Homepage

I am from Vietnam, a wonderful country with friendly people, diverse culture, beautiful nature, and amazing food. Please enjoy my website and copyright pictures/videos throughout these pages.

If you come to Vietnam, most likely you will fly into Ho Chi Minh City. It is the largest city situated in the South of Vietnam and is also known as Saigon. The streets are insanely clog of motorbikes but there’s so much to love about this city, including museums, world-class restaurants, luxury hotels, and best of all, street food stalls.

First and foremost, you must try Street Food - delicious, cheap, and authentic foods on the city’s sidewalks and alleys.

You can find lots of tropical fruits at the local markets or fruit shops along the street. My favorites are Mangosteen, Dragon Fruit Longan, and Rambutan.

The traffic of Saigon is an unforgettable experience, so just take a deep breath before crossing the street.

Believe it or not, you can actually fit a family of 5 on a scooter!

You can get away from the hectic pace and traffic madness of Saigon and visit my Favorite Places in the Northern Vietnam.
